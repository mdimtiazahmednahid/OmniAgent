Project Title: Omnigent an AI Agent plaform primarily responsible for managing omni-channel messages, help in order, customer to get the right direction, business analytics and easy web/app integration like crisp. 


Core Features:

Omni-channel support: Seamlessly manage messages from multiple channels including WhatsApp, Facebook Messenger, Instagram Direct Messages, and web/mobile SDKs.

AI-powered responses: Leverage large language models (LLMs) to understand customer queries and provide accurate, context-aware responses.

Order management: Track and manage orders directly within the platform, allowing agents to assist customers with order-related inquiries.

Workflow automation: Automate routine tasks and workflows, freeing up agents to focus on more complex issues.

Business analytics: Gain valuable insights into customer interactions, agent performance, and business trends through comprehensive analytics and reporting.

Easy integration: Provide simple and flexible integration options for web and mobile applications, enabling businesses to embed the platform seamlessly into their existing infrastructure.
